  • i am a serial hyperfixator. here's some of my hobbies and passions:
    • i listen to a lot of music and i DJ for fun with my friends On Line. i like any music that sounds like it feels good. and i think djing is cool because it can function as a really gorgeous storytelling medium for both the performer and the listener. i'm very inspired by artists like CCL and DJ Voices.
    • i love photography and im extremely drawn to interesting textures. i shoot on a Sony RX100 VI and a Canon SD1300 (the blue one :3) running CHDK. if you want to get into photography you should get the shittiest camera possible because limitations make you better. you should also find a photographer who inspires you and try to replicate their style. i learned a lot from trying to replicate ernst haas' work, even if it's not some of my favorite photography.
    • i love source engine, i've spent thousands of hours bunnyhopping and it means the world to me. it's the most beautiful art form i've ever seen in a videogame and there is almost nothing in this world that compares to the flow state or the feeling you get when you are fully engaged in a run. it's why this website has the url that it has and why i have a tattoo of bunnies hopping on my arm.
    • i love cooking and i love coffee. i'm most familiar with chinese or korean cuisine and i make a killer suan la fen. i make pour over coffee every day and i adore naturally processed light roasted beans that taste like fruit.
